About Me

I am Misbah, a UI/UX Designer based in Banda Aceh but open to relocation.
I graduated with a Electrical Engineering degree from Syiah Kuala University,
I then worked at PT. Mitra Petra Sejahtera as Merchandiser. As a field worker,
I gained valuable skills such as understanding user needs then making some considerations for problem solving. I enjoyed working as a Merchandiser, but wanted to work more in line with the field of study. I then came across UI/UX Design, which I realised is basically a perfect fit for me. I then took a UI/UX Research & Design bootcamp in Binar Academy and just finished it a few months ago.
